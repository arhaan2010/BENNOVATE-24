About the Project

Sahayak is a visionary project designed to empower individuals from rural areas and rural-to-urban migrants by equipping them with skills, tools, and resources necessary for personal and professional growth. The project addresses the socio-economic challenges faced by these communities, providing solutions through technology, mentorship, and accessibility.

Core Features

  • Tutorials in regional languages (English, Hindi, or dialects) with a conversational focus.
  • Self-confidence boosting activities and community connections.
  • Financial literacy courses on savings, budgeting, and loans.
  • Short lessons on skills like self-defense, coding, and entrepreneurship.

Join the Sahayak Movement

Sahayak is more than an app; it's a transformative movement designed to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity. By fostering inclusivity, economic independence, and long-term sustainability, Sahayak empowers individuals with tools, certified courses, and community connections.
